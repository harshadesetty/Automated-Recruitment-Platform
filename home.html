B.Tech Programme

Initiated in 2017, this four-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programme in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) aims to develop high-quality professionals with strong industry relevance.
The programme offers three distinct courses:
- Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering (BCS)
- Bachelor of Technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Bachelor of Technology in Mathematics & Scientific Computing (MSC)

Key Competencies:
  • Strong computer science fundamentals (Operating System, Computer Networks)
  • Good problem solving skills with strong hold on Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • Development (Android Stack, MERN Stack, Python Stack, JAVA)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis.
  • Blockchain and Web 3.0 development.
  • Database and Cloud Native technologies.

Integrated Post Graduate Programme

This is a 5-year course leading to Dual Degree in Information Technology (IT). Under this programme two distinct courses are offered, namely:
- B.Tech (IT) + M.Tech
- B.Tech (IT) + MBA

Key Competencies:
    B.Tech(IT) + M.Tech(IT)
  • Strong aptitude and analytical skills
  • Full Stack Development (Application, MERN, Python, JAVA, Golang)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis
  • Blockchain and Web 3.0 development
  • Cloud-Native Technologies (Kubernetes, Observability)
    B.Tech(IT) + MBA
  • Full Stack Development (Application, MERN, Python, JAVA, Golang)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Business Analytics, Finance, Operations
  • Strong leadership and managerial skills

Post Graduate M.Tech

This is a 2-year program leading to an M.Tech. degree in the area of:
- Wireless Networks and Computing (WNC)
- Information and Cybersecurity (ICS)- Very Large Scale integration (VLSI) and Embedded Systems.

Key Competencies:
  • System on Chip (SOC)
  • Network on Chip (NOC)
  • Massive MIMO Communication
  • Antenna design for wireless
  • Communication DRA and DRO antenna
  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things
  • Multiple access techniques for 5G
  • Infrastructure design, setup, configuration (CN), Redundant Systems (CN)
  • Performance matching (CN)

RECRUITMENT PROCESS

  • INVITATION

    Institute's CDP Cell invites companies with relevant info and documents. Companies can also show interest by writing to placement@iiitm.ac.in.

  • RESPONSE

    Institute's CDP Cell responds to companies' ERF within 2-3 days. Campus interview dates are based on the ERF info provided

  • DATES

    Institute decides PPT date after discussions with companies, notifies students after confirmation. PPT and recruitment can happen simultaneously.

  • REGISTRATION

    Institute students register for companies online or by hard copies. After the deadline, the info is sent to companies.

  • SHORTLIST

    Companies are required to send the list of shortlisted students by email to the T&P Cell prior to the campus visit for final interviews.

  • EVALUATION

    Companies visit the institute on allotted dates and conduct selection procedure including GD, Aptitude/Technical tests, and interviews.

  • SELECTION

    Companies must provide final selected student list on the same day to avoid selecting students already selected by other visiting companies.

  • CONCLUSION

    Institute's CDP Cell decides dates, companies must follow allotted time slots, notify of any changes in advance.Video conferencing available for remote interviewing.
